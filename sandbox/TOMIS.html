Explore Jackson Hole From the Snake River

Enjoy the abundant wildlife with the Teton Mountain Range as a backdrop while your experienced guide does all the work! Bald eagles, pelicans, osprey, blue herons, and sandhill cranes are a few of the birds that are regularly seen on our trips. River otters, beavers, deer, elk, and moose also frequent the area. Water, sparkling water, and binoculars are provided for maximum comfort. Come explore the Snake River on a scenic float trip today!

Why Us

At Snake River Float Tours, we're proud to offer fun, family-friendly river adventures with safety and comfort at the heart of everything we do. Our custom rafts feature high-back cushioned seats, beverages, and binoculars to help you relax and take in the stunning scenery of Jackson Hole.

As a locally owned and operated company, our passionate guides bring years of experience and a love for the outdoors to every trip. Private floats are available, and every outing is designed to be safe, memorable, and full of joy.
